# Account & Data Deletion Request for FluenScene

FluenScene respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data, you can do so at any time.

## Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)
If you currently have the FluenScene app installed on your device, you can delete your data instantly:
1. Open FluenScene and ensure you are signed in.
2. Navigate to the **Settings** screen (gear icon on the home screen).
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account & Personal Data**.
4. Tap **Confirm Delete**.
*This will instantly and permanently purge your Google Sign-In connection, fluency rank, and localization preferences from our Firebase databases.*

## Option 2: Web Deletion Request (Without the App)
If you no longer have the app installed and wish to request the deletion of your account, you can submit a web request. 

Please send an email to **contactsanjaygireeshisme@gmail.com** with the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request - FluenScene"**. 

In the body of the email, please include:
* The email address associated with your Google Sign-In account used for FluenScene.

### What Data Will Be Deleted?
Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently purged from our servers within 7 days:
* Your associated Email Address and User ID.
* Your AI Fluency Level and API translation history.
* Your cultural localization and region preferences.
* Your premium subscription status mapping.

*Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel any active Google Play Billing subscriptions. You must manage your subscriptions directly through the Google Play Store app.*
